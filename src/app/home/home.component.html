About the Company

In 1991, a group of 4 Computer Science students at Arkansas Tech University had a dream. Their dream was to Secure all Wealth Over Land. But, how did they do that? At this point, you know the answer. SWOL was birthed into the world of secure transportation. Based out of Fort Smith, Arkansas, SWOL will deliver to and from every state in the greatest country on Earth! Whether it be gold, cash, jewelry, or some other valuable, you can count on us to get it to you in a stylish, yet secure, fashion!

SWOL is not only our company name, it is an accurate description of our employees.
